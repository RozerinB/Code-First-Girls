How to make Patatas Bravas with Chorizo

Quick and easy - perfect for the whole family

Serves 6 as an appetiser. Would recommend serving with fresh bread or other tapas dishes.

Equipment Needed

  • Frying pan
  • Peeler
  • Knife
  • Wooden Spoon

Ingredients

  1. 1kg Potatoes
  2. 400g Chopped Tomatoes (can)
  3. 2 Garlic Cloves
  4. 1 Onion
  5. 300g Chorizo Sausage
  6. Paprika
  7. Cayenne Pepper Spice
  8. Olive Oil

Method

  1. Chop onion and garlic into small pieces.
  2. Prepare and chop potatoes into small chunks. Slice chorizo.
  3. Heat a little oil in the frying pan and fry onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add tomatoes and season well with paprika and cayenne pepper. Cook for around 20 minutes to allow sauce to thicken.
  5. Steam potatoes for 10 minutes
  6. Fry chorizo. Use separate pan to allow the sausage to cook.
  7. Add potatoes and chorizo to frying pan and mix everything together.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
