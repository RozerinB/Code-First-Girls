Mirza Ghasemi

Mirza Ghassemi or Mirza Qassemi is an Iranian appetizer or main based on tandoori or grilled aubergine, distinct to the Northern Iran and Caspian Sea region. It is known as Persian Eggplant Dip in Western countries.

Equipment needed:

  • Saucepan
  • Fork
  • Pan
  • Oven
  • Wooden spoon
  • Plate
  • Oven mittens
  • Sharp Knife
  • TableSpoon
  • Teaspoon

Ingredients:

  • 1 aubergine
  • 4 diced cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • Fresh Basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Stab the entire surface of 1 whole aubergine so the inside will cook
  2. Put the aubergine in the oven and let it cook for 30 minutes at 180 degrees celsius
  3. Heat up a pan and place 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  4. Add 4 cloves of finely diced garlic into the pan
  5. Get 1 full can od chopped tomaties and add that onto the pan
  6. Mix it well
  7. Get thr aubergine to the pan and mix well
  8. Add the aubergine to the pan and mix well
  9. Let it cool down and serve with rice or naan bread if you're having it for lunch or dinner

TIP

After step 10, you can add upto 2 eggs and scramble it into the mixture after you've let it cool down for a bit if you don't want it to be vegan and add more protein.
